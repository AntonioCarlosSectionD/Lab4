Biography

My name is Antonio Carlos. I was born on July 2, 005.M42 on the Deathworld of Catachan. I was the lucky quarter of infants who was able to live past 10. My mother and father spent their lives fighting the deadly fauna and flora of my homeplanet, and so will I. I was stationed with the 143rd Siege Regiment of the Death Korps of Krieg on the planet of Najan. The planet was cleared of all life after being infected by deadly pathogens couple hundred years ago. After colonization, the planet was attacked again by the Greenskin menance, the Orks. I didn't know a pathogen can be deadly enough to warrant its extermination, and I didn't knew one was real. A buddy of mine said it was caused by those from the 14th Legion. I never new a 14th Legion even existed. He was later summoned by the Commissariate and I never saw him again. I miss him.
